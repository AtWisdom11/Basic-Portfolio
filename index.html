About Me

Hello, my name is Michael Monreal and I was born and raised in San Antonio Texas. As the cliched kid who loved to take apart the family pc and put it back together, most of my career thus far had me troubleshooting computers. I've worked Helpdesk at places like USAA and CITI, which thought me the value of empathy when putting out fires. Currently, I work as a Deskside technician at Boeing Port San Antonio. I finally get to take apart computers and put them back together while actually getting paid to do it! But I wanted more. So I signed up for the Coding Bootcamp at UT Austin. Which empowered me on my quest for world domination.
